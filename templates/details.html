Blood Pressure Stages

Understanding the different categories of blood pressure readings

Normal

Systolic less than 120 AND diastolic less than 80 mmHg

Elevated

Systolic 120-129 AND diastolic less than 80 mmHg

Hypertension Stage 1

Systolic 130-139 OR diastolic 80-89 mmHg

Hypertension Stage 2

Systolic 140/90 mmHg or higher

Hypertensive Crisis

Systolic higher than 180 AND/OR diastolic higher than 120 mmHg
